So, what is it all about?

I'd like to introduce bookmarks to you. Ah, you're familiar with it?! Let me rephrase then. I'd like to show you what bookmarks of the future are gonna be:

  • No secrets. Does Google track your bookmarks through Chrome? I don't know, but here you can learn how MarkALink works. Contribute? Go ahead!
  • Simple... You can just sort links into the groups, so that would make no difference from default bookmarks in any browser. Add current page by clicking the extension's icon or mark a link from context menu (right click). That's simplicity.
  • ... and deep at the same time. Make yourself notice marked links in the page through the custom styling. Make pages you don't want to open next time disappear. Add a reminder to a link: «read this important article», «to wish a happy birthday», «don't forget about this album's release». You can do a lot with it!
  • Made with Love. This website and extension itself are made by a guy, who wants to live in a better world. This project is non-commercial, creating all of this requires a lot of time. Why am I telling you this? Nothing but love and faith in my mission made it possible.
